## Hello, I'm Hyunmin

I'm a Product Designer interested in **B2B SaaS**, **AI/CRM UX**, **prototyping**, and **product analytics**.

I like to connect user context, product data, information architecture, prototyping, handoff, and post-launch validation into one product improvement process.

- I frame ambiguous problems into clear product directions.
- I use data as evidence, but also check how the data was measured.
- I design AI features by thinking about trust, scope, states, and exceptions.
- I enjoy turning complex workflows into simple, usable interfaces.
- I majored in **Global Media** at **Soongsil University**.
